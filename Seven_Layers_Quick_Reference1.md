## The Eight Layers at a Glance

| # | Layer | What it covers | Where programmes get stuck |
|---|-------|---------------|---------------------------|
| 0 | **Economic Viability** | Does this asset need to be tokenised? Sustainable investor demand and viable economics? | Teams build technically perfect platforms with no investor demand. Layer 0 is skipped in most failed programmes. |
| 1 | **Asset** | Legal ownership, due diligence, independent valuation, SPV formation | Hidden encumbrances discovered at notarial stage — after technical work has started on wrong assumptions |
| 2 | **Legal & Regulatory** | Token classification, CASP assessment, jurisdictional analysis, signed legal opinion | Wrong token class = full architectural redesign. CASP: 3–6 month timeline, never on the plan |
| 3 | **Tokenisation** | Smart contract design and audit, token standard, whitelist, oracle, Proof of Reserves | Audit finding post-code-freeze = new audit cycle. PoR not in original architecture = migration |
| 4 | **Financial Infrastructure** | SPV banking, custody model, settlement, ISO 20022 integration | Bank refuses SPV — programme stops. Custody model decided too late to change |
| 5 | **Identity & Compliance** | KYC/AML workflow, investor onboarding, CASP licensing, ongoing monitoring | Onboarding bottleneck becomes go-live blocker. Revenue delayed 3–6 months |
| 6 | **Market & Liquidity** | Secondary market architecture, minimum viable liquidity threshold, yield distribution | Platform launches without buyers. Investors cannot exit. Trust collapses |
| 7 | **Governance & Reporting** | Decision rights, governance charter, audit trail — **runs across ALL layers from day one** | Governance vacuum post-launch. No clear owner when things go wrong |

---

## The Cascade Principle

All eight layers are active **simultaneously**. A change in one cascades to all others.

This is why sequential thinking — build first, add compliance later — fails every time in RWA programmes.

> *"The most dangerous assumption in RWA delivery is: 'We will address that later.' In an eight-layer programme, 'later' always means 'after a dependency we didn't see has already created a delay.'"*

---

## Three Risks Every PM Must Know

**CASP Licensing** — 3–6 months. Must be on the critical path from Week 1, not after the platform is built.

**Whitelist Management** — not a one-time setup. A 24/7 operational process. AML flag = 24-hour removal SLA.

**Proof of Reserves** — MiCA Article 76 obligation. Must be built into the smart contract from day one — not added post-launch.
